SkyVue

To enable tracing in Skyvue, add the following line to the SkyVue.ini file, where <package> represent the specific package level where tracing is desired. Multiple packages are delimited by a space:

-Dcom.cerner.bedrock.instrument.logging.eclipse.tracing.enabled=" <package> <package> ... "
