BACKGROUND

The goal of Study Validation is to keep the Millennium and Archive data in sync. When Archive receives the study, it send an S-Notify to Millennium and enters an 'Announced' status. Millennium receives the data via S-Notify and attempts to perform an Exact Match (if receiving the Millennium study instance UID) or an AutoMatch (non-Millennium UID, using automatch rules defined to identify matching exams by criteria defined). When the match attempt begins, the Millennium side enters a "Matched Viewable Unconfirmed" state and sends a U-Notify to update the archive with the Millennium data; and remains in this state until receiving confirmation back from Archive that it's in a 'Validated' state.

ITEMS FOR INVESTIGATION

  • Accession Number
  • Study Instance UID (SIUID)

SERVERS/SERVICES INVOLVED

  • SCP 353
  • SCP 354
  • Archive

STEPS

Collect data from the Millennium IM tables for reference (IM_STUDY, IM_ACQUIRED_STUDY)

It is also helpful to have the Match History and U-Notify History for reference (IM_MATCH_EVENT, IM_U_NOTIFY)

Review the VALIDATION_STATUS on the archive for reference.

If Archive still has 'Announced' status: open MatchListMgmt.exe, find procedure and click the "Validate" button. If it does not process successfully, review the 354 logs for cause of failure (354 logs because you're manually resubmitting the U-Notify via utility). You should see the U_NOTIFY_ID in the transaction.

If Archive has 'Validated' status: open MatchListMgmt.exe, find procedure and click the "Validate" button. Right-click the row and select "Set to Done". If this fails, review 354 logs for cause of failure. (354 logs because you're manually resubmitting the U-Notify via utility).

If the above do not resolve the issue, further investigation must be performed to identify why Millennium/Archive are out of sync.
